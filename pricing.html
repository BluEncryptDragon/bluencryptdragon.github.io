Determination of Applicable Service Rate

The applicable service rate is determined by the required response time, urgency of attendance, and operational impact, as assessed by ProSolv IT.

  • Rapid Response: Requests requiring immediate or priority attendance will be classified as Rapid Response
  • Standard After-Hours: Requests that can be scheduled without immediate attendance may be serviced under standard after-hours rates

ProSolv IT will confirm the applicable rate prior to attendance or commencement of work.

Examples of Rapid Response Situations

Rapid Response may apply to situations including, but not limited to:

  • Business-critical system or server outages
  • Network or internet failures impacting operations
  • Email or cloud service outages
  • Security incidents or suspected security breaches
  • Any request requiring immediate or same-day priority attendance outside normal business hours

Changes in Urgency or Scope

If, upon on-site or remote assessment, the urgency, impact, or scope of the issue differs from the information initially provided, ProSolv IT reserves the right to reclassify the service and apply the appropriate rate.

In such cases, the client will be notified, and approval will be obtained before further work is carried out.

Additional Information

Return Visits: If we need to return for the same issue within 7 days, no additional callout fee is charged (hourly rates apply).

Travel: Free within Christchurch city. Additional charges apply for Selwyn/Rolleston/Lincoln (+$20), Rangiora/Kaiapoi (+$30).

Payment: We accept bank transfer, cash. Payment is due upon completion unless otherwise arranged.

Parts & Software: Charged separately at cost plus reasonable markup where applicable.
